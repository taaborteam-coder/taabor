# Taabor User Guide

## Introduction

Welcome to **Taabor**, your ultimate queue management and booking solution. This guide will help you navigate the app features efficiently.

## getting Started

### 1. Account Creation

- Open the app and click "Sign Up".
- Enter your Email, Password, and Phone Number.
- Verify your account via the OTP sent to your phone.

### 2. Finding Businesses

- Use the **Home Screen** to browse categories (Salons, Clinics, Restaurants).
- Use the **Search Bar** to find specific places.
- Use the **Map View** (icon on top right) to find businesses near you.

## Troubleshooting

- **App Crashing?** Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store/Play Store.
- **No Location?** Enable GPS permission in your phone settings.
- **Language?** Change language (Arabic/English/Kurdish) in **Settings**.
