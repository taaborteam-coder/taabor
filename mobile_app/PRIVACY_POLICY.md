# Privacy Policy - Taabor

**Last Updated**: December 15, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to Taabor ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number
- **Profile Information**: Profile picture, preferences
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates to find nearby businesses
- **Payment Information**: Payment methods (processed securely via third-party providers)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, features used, time spent
- **Location Data**: Real-time location when using map features
- **Analytics Data**: App performance, crashes, errors

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Service Delivery**: Provide queue management and booking services
- **Personalization**: Customize your experience and recommendations
- **Communication**: Send notifications about your bookings and queue status
- **Analytics**: Improve our services and user experience
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
- **Legal Compliance**: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

## Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

- **Businesses**: When you join their queue or make a booking
- **Service Providers**: Firebase (Google), payment processors
- **Analytics Partners**: Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights

We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication via Firebase
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Opt-Out**: Disable location services or notifications
- **Data Portability**: Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at <privacy@taabor.com>

## Location Data

We collect location data to:

- Show nearby businesses
- Calculate distances and wait times
- Provide navigation features

You can disable location services in your device settings, but some features may not work properly.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Firebase**: Authentication, database, analytics
- **Google Maps**: Location and mapping services
- **Payment Processors**: Secure payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Data Retention

- Your account is active
- Needed to provide services

You can request deletion at any time.
